ABOUT US

About our company: we are located in China, the province of Wuhan, named after the one where the first outbreak of the epidemic began. After the pandemic left our province, all production and normal life of people was adjusted in a short time for quick "inclusion in the work", because we have one of the factories for the production of medical masks and respirators. We are a subsidiary of the plant, created to provide assistance at the non-state level to fellow Europeans in the preparation of personal protective equipment during the pandemic. Your parcel will be sent directly from us to Your address. On the date of delivery: After placing and paying for the order, we have 24 hours to send it to You, otherwise the order will be canceled and a refund will be made. We usually ship orders twice a day: at 10 am and 10 PM. Your order will be very fast, because we cooperate with aliexpress delivery and can send a parcel to any country in the world on the day of order. Usually the delivery time takes up to a week, on average from 3 to 5 days. About order size: If you want to order more than a thousand pieces per order, it is not possible to do so. After the introduction of certain rules in Wuhan province, we can not export personal protective equipment in the amount of more than 1000 pieces in one parcel, so You can make several orders to the same address and we will send You your parcels.
